### 👋 About Me

- 🎓 PhD Candidate in **Economics** at **Newcastle University Business School**
- 🔬 I work on **applied microeconomics** — the economics of **migration, crime, and the labour market**
- 📊 Empirical researcher: causal inference with large administrative and survey datasets
- 🌍 Based in Newcastle upon Tyne, United Kingdom
- 🗣️ Turkish (native) · English (fluent) · German (fluent)

### 🔍 Research Interests

`Economics of Migration`  ·  `Economics of Crime`  ·  `Labour Economics`  ·  `Applied Microeconometrics`  ·  `Forced Migration & Integration`
